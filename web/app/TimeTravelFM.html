Time Travel FM

Designed with elements from periods of the 20th century, bridging current technology with the past. Travel in time with you radio; dial back to relive moments of a chosen year.

Why?

Music is the language of emotion! Time Travel FM taps into nostalgia. Feel the past with an old song. Experience a period through the voice and language of vintage commercials.

Features

Physical Interface

Select a year and mood by turning a physical dial.

Playlists by year and mood

The dial controls the Time Travel mobile app, which plays different playlists from your Spotify account.

Vintage Commercials

We threw old commercial into the mix enhacing your feel for the era.

Customize

Our playlists are editable giving you the ability to perfect the experience.

How does it work?

We took a flux capicator and attached it to an antenna. Because it only has to pick up radio waves, it doesn't need 1.8 gigawatts of electricity, or need to travel at 88 mph.
All you have to do is dock your iPhone through the side.
